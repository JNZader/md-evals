# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free community experience for all contributors and users of md-evals, regardless of:

- Age
- Body size
- Disability
- Ethnicity
- Gender identity and expression
- Level of experience
- Education
- Nationality
- Personal appearance
- Race
- Religion
- Sexual identity and orientation
- Socioeconomic status

We believe that a diverse and inclusive community strengthens our project and creates better tools for everyone.

## Our Standards

### Expected Behavior

We expect all community members to:

✅ **Be respectful and inclusive**
- Use welcoming and inclusive language
- Welcome differing viewpoints and experiences
- Recognize that we can learn from each other
- Respect different communication styles and needs

✅ **Be professional and collaborative**
- Focus on the work, not the person
- Give credit where it's due
- Assume good intent in interactions
- Ask clarifying questions rather than making assumptions

✅ **Be constructive**
- Provide feedback that helps improve the project
- Help others succeed and grow
- Report genuine issues through proper channels
- Celebrate others' contributions and wins

✅ **Be trustworthy**
- Keep discussions confidential when appropriate
- Protect others' privacy
- Be honest and transparent
- Follow through on commitments

### Unacceptable Behavior

We do not tolerate:

❌ **Harassment**
- Unwelcome sexual comments, advances, or attention
- Intimidation or threats
- Sustained, targeted criticism designed to discourage
- Stalking or following
- Any other form of harassment

❌ **Discrimination and Bigotry**
- Derogatory comments based on identity
- Exclusion based on characteristics listed above
- Using slurs or dehumanizing language
- Promoting discrimination
- Denying the dignity of others

❌ **Abuse and Hostility**
- Insulting or demeaning language
- Excessive profanity or aggressive tone
- Personal attacks
- Dismissive behavior toward others
- Deliberate exclusion

❌ **Manipulation and Bad Faith**
- Spreading misinformation
- Deliberately derailing discussions
- Bad faith arguments
- Doxxing or sharing private information
- Impersonation or deception

❌ **Other Violations**
- Spam or self-promotion
- Advocating for or encouraging any of the above
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Other inappropriate conduct

## Where This Applies

This Code of Conduct applies to all spaces under md-evals community control:

- GitHub repositories, issues, and discussions
- Pull request comments and reviews
- Code comments in contributions
- Project documentation
- Community events and forums
- Social media when representing the project
- Public communications about the project
- Project mailing lists

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all people who:
- Contribute code or documentation
- Participate in discussions or issues
- Attend community events
- Represent the project in any capacity

It applies equally to maintainers and users.

## Reporting Issues

### For Non-Emergency Issues

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it:

**GitHub Issues (preferred for conduct violations):**
- Open a private issue on GitHub if available
- Or contact maintainers directly

**Email:**
- Contact: javier@example.com
- Subject line: "Code of Conduct Violation Report"
- Include: Date, location, description, names if applicable

**What to Include:**
- Description of what happened
- Who was involved (if comfortable sharing)
- When and where it occurred
- Impact on you or others
- Any additional context

### For Security or Safety Issues

⚠️ **If you feel unsafe or threatened, contact local authorities immediately.**

For security vulnerabilities, see [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).

### Confidentiality

All reports will be:
- Treated confidentially
- Handled promptly
- Investigated fairly
- Protected from retaliation

Reporters should expect:
- Initial acknowledgment within 48 hours
- Status updates within one week
- Final resolution within reasonable time
- Explanation of actions taken (to extent possible)

## Enforcement

### Response Process

When we receive a report, we will:

1. **Acknowledge** receipt within 48 hours
2. **Investigate** thoroughly and fairly
3. **Determine** if Code of Conduct violation occurred
4. **Respond** with appropriate action
5. **Follow up** to ensure resolution

### Enforcement Actions

Responses may include (in increasing severity):

**1. Warning**
- For minor or first-time violations
- Clear explanation of unacceptable behavior
- Expectation of improvement

**2. Temporary Suspension**
- For repeated violations or serious incidents
- Duration may range from days to weeks
- Conditions for reinstatement

**3. Permanent Ban**
- For severe, repeated, or unresolved violations
- May include removal of contributions
- Removal from all project spaces

**4. Additional Actions**
- Action on external platforms if appropriate
- Law enforcement involvement if necessary
- Community notification about serious violations

Maintainers have discretion to take appropriate action including banning individuals from all project spaces.

### No Retaliation

We prohibit retaliation against anyone who:
- Reports violations in good faith
- Participates in investigations
- Advocates for this Code of Conduct

Retaliation is a serious violation itself.

## Transparency

To maintain trust, we will:
- Document significant Code of Conduct incidents
- Share aggregate statistics
- Review and improve our processes
- Be transparent about enforcement actions when appropriate

## Appeals

If you believe action taken was unjust, you can:

1. Request a review within 30 days
2. Provide additional context or evidence
3. Request a discussion with maintainers

Appeals will be:
- Reviewed by different maintainers if possible
- Decided within two weeks
- Final
